Blunts Bridge Estates

Open in Google Maps

Discover Blunts Bridge Estates, an exclusive new community by The Kittrell Company nestled in the heart of Hanover County. Designed for those who value privacy without compromise, this neighborhood features just ten premier homesites—each spanning over 10 sprawling acres.

Enjoy a true natural sanctuary with signature lots that extend directly to the water's edge along the South Anna River, or design your custom retreat tucked away in a lush, wooded setting. While the atmosphere is purely rural, the conveniences of Downtown Ashland—with its local boutiques, dining, and charm—are just a short, five-mile drive away.

At Blunts Bridge, the vision is yours. Choose from one of Kittrell's curated floor plans or bring your own custom design to life. Call for more details.

Belmont Green

Open in Google Maps

Discover Belmont Green, the newest boutique community by The Kittrell Company in the heart of Henrico County. Tucked away in a private, serene setting, this neighborhood offers a rare opportunity to live alongside the beautifully restored Belmont Golf Course.

Experience the very best of Lakeside living. Whether you're exploring the world-class blooms at Lewis Ginter Botanical Garden, cycling the upcoming Fall Line Trail, or enjoying the local favorites that make this community so unique, Belmont Green puts you at the center of it all.

With only 18 lots available, this is a limited opportunity to build your future in one of the area's most desired locations.



Meet the Builders

The Kittrell Company, a family-owned business, has been building homes and developing neighborhoods in the Richmond area for over 35 years. Each build incorporates smart design and superior craftsmanship supported by a firm foundation of honesty.

Kittrell understands the importance of good communication and strives to maintain a close relationship with their clients. As a small volume builder, they are able to listen to your ideas and meet every individuals' need. You deserve that attention to detail when having a home built.

Kittrell knows customer service produces customer satisfaction. Before, during, and after the sale they will exceed expectations and provide you the with best service possible. Let them show you how committed they are to building your dreams. They are waiting to hear from you.
